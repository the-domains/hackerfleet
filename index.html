MS Stubnitz, from London to Dunkirk,

This timelapses covers the first and last 3h of the journey of MS Stubnitz from London, Canary Wharf to Dunkirk, France. This journey took place from 05-17-2013 17.00 UTC to 05-18-2013 12.30 UTC It was shot with two HD GoPro, one central at the monkeydeck looking forward and the other one above the quarter deck on starboard side.
